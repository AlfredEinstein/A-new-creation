Key Accountant Responsibilities

The main responsibilities of an accountant are distributed in accordance with the position
and scale of the business.

Primary accounting collection, processing of acts, accounts, business contracts, waybills, etc.
Maintaining accounting registers, accounting programs, the reflection of operations on the accounts of the organization.
Accounting, write-off, depreciation on fixed assets, participation in inventories.
Conducting cash transactions, communication with banks and creditors.
Payroll, accrual of sick leave, leave, compensation, preparation of payroll, control of salary payments, forecasting payroll.
Formation of the tax base, calculation of insurance premiums and payments, transfer to funds.
Preparation and transfer of accounting, tax, statistical reporting. Interaction with inspection bodies, provision of data.
